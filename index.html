About

I love stories. I love reading them and I love making them up. To put it in a book of course, not to spread rumours. Unless of course, the character is a Tilly Tattle. My name is Kat, and I am a writer, creator, and in the process of learning how to code. For years I worked in logistics, solving practical problems and helping keep things moving. Outside of work, however, I have always been building things of a different kind: stories, worlds, videos, and other creative projects. I recently completed my debut fantasy novel and am currently preparing it for publication. Alongside writing, I am teaching myself web development and Python, documenting the journey through videos, articles and personal projects. Whether I’m writing code, working on my story, filming a study session, or creating an entire fantasy world from scratch, I am happiest when I’m learning something new and when I’m in creation mode. This website is my corner of the internet, a place I’d like to share. You are very welcome!

The brave new world we chose | An essay

In the 21st century, We’re not waiting for dystopia. We are already there. Not the loud, boot-stomping dystopia Orwell warned us of (although that does exist in parts of the world), but the quiet, seamless, painless dystopia Huxley feared: where chains are soft and sweet, screens are warm, and sedation is voluntary. It's dictatorship without tears.
